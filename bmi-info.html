What BMI does NOT tell you

BMI does not measure body fat directly, and it does not consider:

  • Muscle mass
  • Bone density
  • Age or gender
  • Overall lifestyle or fitness level

This means two people with the same BMI can have very different bodies and health profiles.

A gentle reminder

Health is more than a number. BMI can be a helpful starting point, but it should never define your worth or well-being.

If you have concerns about your health, consider speaking with a qualified healthcare professional.
